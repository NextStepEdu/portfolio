KindTouch

Open KindTouch in full-screen mode for the best experience.

Launch KindTouch
Use KindTouch like an app

You can add KindTouch to your device’s home screen for easy access.

  • iPhone / iPad: Open KindTouch → tap the Share icon → Add to Home Screen
  • Android: Open KindTouch → tap the menu (⋮) → Add to Home screen

This adds KindTouch to your home screen and opens it in full screen, just like an app — without using the App Store. Nothing is downloaded or installed — it simply opens KindTouch in a cleaner, full-screen view.
