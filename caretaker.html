KindTouch – Caregiver Guide

KindTouch is a calm, caregiver-guided activity set designed for people living with bv-FTD and related conditions.

General Principles

  • No right or wrong answers
  • Avoid failure states or scoring
  • Encourage engagement without pressure
  • Support familiarity, recognition, and gentle creativity
  • Short, relaxed sessions work best
  • The caregiver controls navigation

Helpful tip: KindTouch can be added to the tablet or phone’s Home Screen via the browser menu, making it easy to reopen like an app.

Activities

Colouring: Tap areas to add colour.

Arrange: Drag items into place.

Name the Person: Tap a person to reveal their name.

Caregiver Controls

Caregiver Menu - Quickly tap five times in the top-right corner. Options include:

  • Retry (clears items)
  • Allow two user buttons (Home and next image)
  • Next / Previous image
  • Start Activity
  • Exit to main menu

If an activity becomes tiring or frustrating, simply exit and return later.

Tips for Best Use

  • Sit alongside the person where possible
  • Allow them to interact at their own pace
  • Avoid giving instructions unless needed
  • Use calm, reassuring language
  • It’s okay if they simply watch or tap briefly

Important Notes

  • KindTouch does not collect personal data
  • Progress is not scored or judged
  • The app is intended as a supportive activity, not a medical tool

Final Thought

  • KindTouch is about presence, not performance.
  • Even a few moments of calm interaction can be meaningful.
