KindTouch is a calm, caregiver-guided activity set designed for people living with bv-FTD and related conditions.

General Principles

  • No right or wrong answers
  • Short, relaxed sessions work best
  • The caregiver controls navigation

Activities

Colouring: Tap areas to add colour.

Arrange: Drag items into place.

Name the Person: Tap a person to reveal their name.

If an activity becomes tiring or frustrating, simply exit and return later.
