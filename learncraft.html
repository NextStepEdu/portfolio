Learn Craft

One-on-One Interactive Learning

Learn Craft is an interactive one-on-one experience designed for teachers with their own clientele base. It transforms a standard online lesson into an engaging, gamified experience.

Explore Virtual Environments

Teachers and learners explore rich 3D environments together in real time. The lesson feels immersive and collaborative, encouraging students to participate more actively.

Real-Time Custom Content

Teachers can upload their own materials—including text, images, audio, and questions—at runtime, allowing for completely personalized and adaptable lesson content.

Platform Requirements

Students will need a client version of Learn Craft installed on their Windows or Mac device. The app is optimized for smooth remote use and includes teacher controls.
