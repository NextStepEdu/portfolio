Lounges

Your Shared Creative Space

Lounges is a shared online whiteboard where up to three people can create, draw and explore together in real time.

Sketch ideas, add text, decorate your space with interactive objects, or simply enjoy spending time together in your own virtual lounge.

With themed lighting, collaborative drawing, and built-in games like Pictionary, every session becomes a creative experience.

Use It Anywhere

Open Lounges in any modern browser and invite up to two other people to join your space from anywhere in the world.

Whether you're brainstorming ideas, teaching a lesson, playing games or simply catching up, Lounges makes collaboration simple and fun.

Who Uses Lounges?

Lounges brings people together in a shared creative space for learning, collaboration, games and social interaction.

👩‍🏫 Teachers

Run collaborative lessons, brainstorm ideas, draw diagrams, present concepts and engage learners in shared activities.

👨‍👩‍👧 Families & Friends

Meet in your own virtual lounge to draw together, play Pictionary, decorate rooms and simply spend quality time together.

🌍 Remote Teams

Sketch ideas, plan projects, share concepts and collaborate visually without installing any software.

🎨 Creative Minds

Create artwork, design layouts, experiment with ideas and customise your own shared space with objects and drawings.

⭐ Everyone

If you enjoy creating, learning or spending time together online, Lounges provides a simple shared space that works on almost any device.

Launch Lounges

Lounges

Open Lounges in full-screen mode for the best experience.

Lounges is designed for use in landscape mode.

Use Lounges like an app

You can add Lounges to your device’s home screen for easy access.

  • iPhone / iPad: Open Lounges → tap the Share icon → Add to Home Screen
  • Android: Open Lounges → tap the menu (⋮) → Add to Home screen
  • An install option may also appear
  • If there is no Add to Home screen option, delete the old Lounges app

This adds Lounges to your home screen and opens it in full screen, just like an app — without using the App Store. Nothing is downloaded or installed — it simply opens Lounges in a cleaner, full-screen view.
