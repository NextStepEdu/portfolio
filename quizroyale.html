Quiz Royale

Multiplayer Exam Prep Adventure

Quiz Royale is an action-packed, multiplayer learning game that transforms exam preparation into an epic adventure. Designed for up to three learners, it combines academic challenge with a gamified, exploratory experience.

Explore and Battle

Learners, guided by the teacher, explore dynamic 3D environments inspired by popular games like League of Legends. Along the way, they face challenges and enemies that can only be defeated by correctly answering questions.

Answer to Advance

Learners must work together to progress. Answering grammar, vocabulary, or exam-based questions unlocks new areas, boosts character abilities, and opens gateways to tougher challenges.

Team-Based Learning

Quiz Royale is ideal for teachers looking to build collaboration and excitement into their sessions. Each learner takes on a role, contributing to the group’s success through academic accuracy and strategic gameplay.
