MVP Connect

Turn Photos Into Living Memories

Create simple, interactive photo albums with voice.

Add your photos, place tags to highlight people or objects, and record a voice message for each image—so every moment comes with a story.

Perfect for families, language learning, and supporting memory through familiar faces and places.

Free to use. No account needed.

Use It Anywhere

No installs. No accounts.

Just open it in a browser and it works—on phones, tablets, or computers.

You can export your albums and move them between devices anytime, so your memories are always within reach.

Why This Project Matters

This app was created from a very personal place.

Half of all donations will support two young girls who will grow up without their mum due to bv-FTD (a form of early-onset dementia).

In many ways, this app is about holding onto the things that matter—voices, faces, and the small moments that make up a life.

If you find this project useful or meaningful, you’re welcome to support it. There’s no pressure—just appreciation.
