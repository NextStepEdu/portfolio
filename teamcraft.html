Team Craft

Designed for Group Learning

Team Craft is a multiplayer learning experience built for teachers who want to engage up to three learners in a collaborative, gamified environment.

Explore and Collaborate

Like Learn Craft, lessons are conducted in real time, but Team Craft is designed for small groups. Learners appear in third-person view and can move freely through an expansive virtual world.

Dynamic 3D Environments

The environment includes dynamic spaces such as a walkable model of the solar system, an in-game cinema for watching videos and displaying images, and other themed areas that encourage exploration and teamwork.

Custom Content, Live Uploads

Teachers can upload custom content at runtime to adapt the experience to their lesson goals. Team Craft is available for both Windows and Mac.
